Our Philosophy

Grown with Intention,
Prepared with Respect

At Hearth & Harvest, we believe the best ingredients don't need to travel far. Our kitchen is a celebration of the Hudson Valley's bounty, where every dish tells a story of the soil, the weather, and the hands that nurtured it.

Led by Chef Elena Rossi, our menu changes weekly to reflect exactly what is being harvested. From heirloom tomatoes still warm from the sun to heritage grains milled just miles away, we honor the seasons in their purest form.
