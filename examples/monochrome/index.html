The Artist

Obsessed with the interplay of structure and shadow.

Elias Thorne is a photographer whose work resides in the quiet spaces of the world. By stripping away color, Thorne forces the viewer to confront the raw form of the subject—whether it be the curve of a concrete wall or the jagged peaks of a mountain range.

Each print is hand-processed in a traditional darkroom, ensuring a tactile depth that digital reproduction cannot mirror.
