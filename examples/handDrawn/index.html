How to brew
like a pro

Quick Tip:

Always use filtered water. If your water tastes funny, your coffee will too! 🚰

Follow the arrows!

01.

The Grind Matters

Coarse for French Press, fine for Espresso. Think of it like sand: too coarse and the water flows too fast, too fine and it gets stuck!

02.

The Bloom

Wet your grounds slightly and wait 30 seconds. Watch the bubbles! That's the coffee breathing out CO2 so it can soak up the water.

03.

Patience is Key

Pour in slow, concentric circles. Imagine you're drawing a spiral that never ends. Don't rush the magic.
