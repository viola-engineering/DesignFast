Our Core Programs

Science-driven initiatives designed to have the most significant impact on ocean health.

Coral Reef Rebirth

We utilize "coral gardening" techniques to transplant resilient coral species back onto degraded reefs, restoring biodiversity and protecting coastlines.

Learn more about Reefs →

Zero Plastic Tide

Working with local communities to implement plastic interception systems in river mouths and organizing large-scale beach cleanup expeditions.

Learn more about Plastic →

Sanctuary Network

Advocating for government policy to establish Marine Protected Areas (MPAs) where fishing and industrial activity are strictly prohibited.

Learn more about Sanctuaries →
